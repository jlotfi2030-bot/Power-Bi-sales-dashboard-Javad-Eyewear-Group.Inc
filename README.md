# Power-Bi-sales-dashboard-Javad-Eyewear-Group.Inc
End-to-end Power BI project analyzing merchandise sales trends, product performance, customer demographics, and geographic insights using DAX, Power Query, and data visualization.

📊 Merchandise Sales Analytics Dashboard – Built with Microsoft Power BI
This project showcases an end-to-end Merchandise Sales Analytics Dashboard developed to evaluate the performance of a newly launched product line in the eyewear industry.
With the introduction of our own merchandise, this dashboard aims to provide data-driven insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency, enabling better strategic decision-making.
________________________________________
🎯 **Business Objective**

After launching a new line of merchandise, the goal of this analysis is to **monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and uncover actionable insights** from sales data.

This dashboard is designed to answer critical business questions such as:

•	What are the **overall sales trends over time**?

•	Which **product categories** generate the highest revenue?

•	What are the **most and least popular products**?

•	How does **geographic location** impact sales performance?

•	What is the effect of **international vs local sales**?

•	Who are the **key customer segments (age groups)**?

•	How do **ratings and customer satisfaction** relate to sales?

•	What trends exist in **shipping costs and operational expenses**?

•	How do **pricing strategies (discounts/promotions)** influence demand?

•	Are there any patterns in **repeat purchases and order behavior**?

**🔍 Dashboard Overview**

The dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of sales performance through the following components:

**1. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

High-level metrics summarizing business performance:

**Total Sales**: 856.46K

Local: 470K (69%)

International: 387K (31%)

**Total Orders**: 7.39K

Male: 70% | Female: 30%

**Total Quantity Sold**: 12.33K

Avg Quantity per Order: 1.67

**Total Shipping Cost**: 108.20K

Avg Shipping Cost: 14.63

**Average Rating**: 3.50 ⭐

Each KPI is supported by **trend indicators (sparklines)** to track performance changes over time.

**2. Sales Trend Analysis**

Time-series comparison of **Local vs International Sales**

Identifies **seasonality, growth patterns, and demand fluctuations**

Helps evaluate the **impact of global expansion**

**3. Product Performance Analysis**

Sales breakdown by **product category (e.g., Takumi, Parts)**

Highlights:

Best-performing categories

Underperforming segments

Enables data-driven product strategy decisions

**4. Customer Demographics**

Sales distribution across age groups (18–24, 25–29, 30–35)

Comparison of local vs international customer behavior

Supports targeted marketing and segmentation

**5. Geographical Insights**

Performance comparison across multiple locations:

North America (e.g., New Jersey, San Francisco)

Europe (e.g., London, Dublin)

Includes:

Total Sales

Orders

Quantity Sold

Month-over-Month (MoM) trends

This helps identify high-performing regions and expansion opportunities.

**6. Shipping & Operational Analysis**

Tracks total and average shipping costs

Evaluates how shipping impacts:

Profitability

Customer purchasing behavior

Supports optimization of logistics strategy

**7. Customer Satisfaction & Ratings**

Average rating visualization (3.50 ⭐)

Enables correlation between:

Customer satisfaction

Sales performance

Helps identify areas for service and product improvement

**8. Interactive Filtering (Slicers)**

Users can dynamically explore the data using:

Year-Month

Product Category

Rating

Location

This allows for deep-dive analysis and customized insights.
